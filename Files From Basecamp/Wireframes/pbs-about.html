About Plan Benefit Services

Plan Benefit Services, Inc. is an employee benefits consulting, management and brokerage firm based out of Columbia, SC. Independently owned and operated since 1986 and built upon a tradition of integrity, industry leadership and excellence. We are committed to delivering tailored benefit solutions, with innovative strategic planning, elite professional services and leading technology-based solutions. Through sound advice and a progressive vision, we help our customers find answers, implement solutions, and mitigate future challenges.

Plan Benefit Services, Inc. has assembled the finest staff of benefits professionals whose experience is matched by their intelligence and integrity. We further arm them with continuous education, training and cutting-edge technical resources. These highly specialized consultants have helped us build our reputation for excellence.
