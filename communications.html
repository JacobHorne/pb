Communication

Employee benefits is by far one of the highest expense for most companies but how well do your employees and their dependents understand the value you provide? The enrollment team at Plan Benefit Services will develop a customized benefit communications strategy designed to effectively reach the different segments of your employee population. Below are a few of the resources our enrollment team offers:

  • face-to-face employee meetings
  • On-line enrollment and education
  • employee emails
  • Posters
  • Payroll stuffers
  • benefit fairs
  • personalized employee booklets
  • call center

These resources allow us to have a tremendous impact on how well benefits are understood, perceived and utilized.
